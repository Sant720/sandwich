# Overview

Welcome! This guide will teach you how to prepare **my favorite sandwich, The Crunchy Kimchi Turkey Sandwich**.  
The documentation follows a concept-first structure designed to walk you from the *why* → *what* → *how* → *final recipe*.

This approach mirrors professional technical documentation practices and is inspired by the sandwich-making concepts demonstrated in the [core principles](/docs/sandwich/core-principles/) section.

{{% hint info %}}
**What this guide leads to:**  
You will progressively learn the reasoning, components, and assembly steps behind the final recipe:  
**The Crunchy Kimchi Turkey Sandwich**.
{{% /hint %}}

## What you'll learn

By following this documentation, you will:

- Understand the **core principles** that make any sandwich great  
- Explore the **components** used in my sandwich and why they work  
- Follow a clear, structured **assembly guide**  
- Prepare the final recipe for **The Crunchy Kimchi Turkey Sandwich**
