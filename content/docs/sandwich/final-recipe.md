# Final recipe: The crunchy kimchi turkey sandwich

This is the complete, polished recipe for **my favorite sandwich**:  
**The Crunchy Kimchi Turkey Sandwich**.

It incorporates all the concepts from the  
[core principles](/docs/sandwich/core-principles/),  
uses the ingredients explained in  
[components](/docs/sandwich/components/),  
and follows the workflow described in the  
[assembly guide](/docs/sandwich/assembly/).

---

## Ingredients (1 sandwich)

- 1 small **ciabatta** roll  
- ½ medium **avocado**, lightly mashed  
- 3–4 slices **roast turkey**  
- 2–3 tbsp **kimchi**, drained if wet  
- Optional:
  - 1 tbsp **Dijonnaise**  
  - 2–3 thin slices **radish**  
  - Small handful fresh **herbs**

---

## Instructions

{{% steps %}}

1. ## Prepare the bread  
   Slice the ciabatta, toast or griddle the interior, and hollow out a small amount from the top half.

2. ## Add the avocado  
   Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the bottom slice and salt lightly.

3. ## Add the turkey  
   Shingle the turkey slices evenly over the avocado.

4. ## Add the kimchi  
   Scatter drained kimchi over the turkey.

5. ## Add optional enhancements  
   Add radish slices, herbs, or a thin layer of Dijonnaise.

6. ## Close and cut  
   Press lightly, slice diagonally, and serve immediately.

{{% /steps %}}

---

## Serving notes

{{% hint success %}}
For best results, wrap the sandwich in parchment and let it rest for **10–15 minutes**.  
This improves cohesion and enhances the final bite.
{{% /hint %}}

Enjoy **The Crunchy Kimchi Turkey Sandwich** — a balanced, flavorful sandwich built on thoughtful technique and purposeful design.
